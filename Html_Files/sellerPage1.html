Steps to Become a Seller

  1. Click the "Register as Seller" button below to access the registration page.
  2. Fill in your Seller Name, Email, and Password in the registration form.
  3. Upload your Aadhar Card as proof of identity.
  4. Upload your Medicine Seller Certification for authentication.
  5. Click the "Register" button to submit your information.
  6. Once your registration is approved, you will receive a confirmation email.
  7. Log in to your seller account using your registered email and password.
  8. You can now start adding and managing your products in the seller dashboard.
